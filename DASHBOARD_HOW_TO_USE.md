## **Step 3: Read the Numbers (What Do They Mean?)**

### **The Big 4 Numbers at the Top**

```
💰 $498.50          🎯 125              📈 0.28x           🔗 $2.50
Total Spend         Conversions         ROAS               Avg CPC

HOW TO READ:
- Spend:        Money spent this week
- Conversions:  Customers who did something (booked, called, etc)
- ROAS:         Money back per dollar spent (0.28x = 28 cents back per $1)
- Avg CPC:      Average cost per click
```

### **What's Good ROAS?**

```
0.10x = Bad (losing money)      ❌
0.15x = Okay (breaking even)    ⚠️
0.20x = Good (making profit)    ✅
0.25x = Great (winning)         🔥
0.35x+ = Excellent (scaling!)   🚀
```

## **The 3 Rules**

### **Rule 1: Higher ROAS = More Budget**
If Google ROAS is 0.30x and Meta is 0.15x:
- Move money from Meta to Google
- Google works 2x better

### **Rule 2: Watch the Trend**
```
📈 Improving  = Getting better, maybe increase budget
➡️ Stable     = Working fine, keep going
📉 Declining  = Getting worse, investigate or pause
```

### **Rule 3: Trust the Email Report**
**Every morning at 9:15 AM you get an email** with:
- What changed
- What's wrong (if anything)
- What to do next

**Read the email first.** Then come here to take action.

## **FAQ**

### **Q: What if I see a ⚠️ Warning?**
**A:** Click it. It tells you exactly what to do.

### **Q: Can I break something?**
**A:** No. Every action is reversible. Change your mind? Change it back.

### **Q: Why did Google ROAS go down?**
**A:** Seasonality, competition, or ad fatigue. The email will explain.

### **Q: When do I adjust budget?**
**A:** When one platform has 2x better ROAS than another, move 10-20% of budget.

### **Q: What's "Cost Per Click"?**
**A:** How much you pay every time someone clicks your ad.
- $0.50 = cheap (tire shop niche)
- $3.00 = expensive (competitive market)

### **Q: How much should each client spend?**
**A:** Whatever you agreed to. Default is $500/month.
- Google: ~$250 (highest ROI)
- Meta: ~150-170 (secondary)
- Yelp: ~100 (testing)

But let AI rebalance daily. It's smarter than us.

## **Troubleshooting**

### **"I can't log in"**
- Check email/password
- Ask for password reset email
- Try incognito mode (clear cache)

### **"Numbers look wrong"**
- Give it 15 seconds to refresh
- Refresh the page
- Check if client has active campaigns

### **"I want to see more details"**
- Click "📊 Full Report" for everything
- Click client name to see their details
- Email report has all the AI insights

### **"The action button didn't work"**
- Refresh the page
- Try again
- Check email for what happened
