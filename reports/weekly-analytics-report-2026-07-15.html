Findings

No production analytics instrumentation. The repo has no PostHog snippet, no Vercel Analytics package, and no Google Analytics/gtag reference in app/ or lib/. The only dependencies are Clerk, Supabase, Anthropic SDK, Next.js, and nodemailer — nothing analytics-related is installed (package.json).

The 3 events look like internal/admin usage, not customer visits. /admin, /admin/login, and /quote in a single session is consistent with someone (the owner or a developer) testing the site, not a customer researching tire service.
